## 📖 The Story
Once upon a time, there was a loyal dog named **Rex** and a curious cat named **Max**, both living happily with their beloved owner. But one fateful day, their world was turned upside down—their owner mysteriously vanished, leaving only a trail of clues. 

With hearts full of worry, Rex and Max decided to set off on an incredible adventure, determined to find their owner. As they journey through dense forests, across sparkling rivers, and over towering mountains, they face many challenges. Rex uses his strength and sharp instincts, while Max relies on agility and clever thinking. Together, they must overcome every obstacle, test their bond, and learn to work together and trust each other more than ever to save the day!

---

## 🎮 Gameplay & Character Mechanics

This is a **2-player local cooperative game** where teamwork is essential. Each hero has distinct strengths, weaknesses, and survival mechanics that must be synchronized to bypass puzzles and dangers.

### 🐱 Cat Max
* **Super Ability:** Can perform high jumps, allowing it to reach elevated platforms with ease.
* **Weakness:** Moves at a slower speed, so you must plan your actions carefully!
* **Life Item:** Eat a fish 🐟

### 🐶 Dog Rex
* **Super Ability:** Has fast movement speed, perfect for dodging fast-moving obstacles and enemies.
* **Weakness:** Low jump height makes it harder to reach high places without help.
* **Life Item:** Eat a piece of meat 🥩

## ⌨️ Game Controls

| Action | 🐱 Cat Max | 🐶 Dog Rex |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move Left** | `A` | `Left Arrow` |
| **Move Right** | `D` | `Right Arrow` |
| **Jump** | `W` | `Up Arrow` |
| **Shoot** | `Left Ctrl` | `Right Ctrl` |

## ⚙️ Key Technical Features

* **Cooperative Design:** Level layouts that force players to rely on each other's unique mechanics to advance.
* **Dynamic Physics & Mechanics:** Smooth movement tracking, shooting capabilities, and targeted item-collection logic.
* **Interactive UI:** Intuitive visual instructions and seamless menu transitions built with Unity's UI system.
